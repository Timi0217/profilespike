## How to Get Your LinkedIn Profile Text

### Step 1: Go to Your LinkedIn Profile
1. Open your browser and go to [linkedin.com](https://linkedin.com)
2. Click on **"Me"** in the top navigation
3. Select **"View profile"**
4. Make sure you're on your complete profile page

### Step 2: Copy Everything
1. **Select All**: Press `Ctrl+A` (Windows) or `Cmd+A` (Mac)
2. **Copy**: Press `Ctrl+C` (Windows) or `Cmd+C` (Mac)
3. All your profile text is now copied!

### Step 3: Analyze in ProfileSpike
1. Go to **LinkedIn Optimizer** in ProfileSpike
2. Click the **"Copy & Paste"** tab (recommended)
3. **Paste** your copied text: `Ctrl+V` or `Cmd+V`
4. Click **"Analyze Profile Text"**
5. Get instant results! âš¡
